How Korami Works

A transparent, non-speculative system designed for sustainability and social impact.

1

Daily Active Engagement = 1 Korami

Users earn exactly one Korami coin per day by actively participating in games, challenges, learning modules, and interactions on the platform. This creates a fixed, predictable issuance system.

2

Attention Generates Real Revenue

User engagement creates legitimate advertising and sponsorship value. This revenue is collected in fiat currency and held in transparent escrow accounts.

3

Korami Has No Cash Value

Korami coins cannot be bought, sold, or converted to cash. They exist solely as verified engagement tokens with no speculative element, eliminating volatility and manipulation risks.

4

Redemption Triggers Real Payments

When users redeem Korami for products or services, Korami uses the reserved fiat revenue to pay partners directly. This creates a closed, transparent flow from attention to real-world value.

Core Technical Principles

Fixed Issuance

1 Korami = 1 active day. No inflation, no arbitrary creation.

Anti-Inflation Logic

Supply is tied directly to verified engagement, not monetary policy.

No Speculation

Cannot be traded or converted to cash. Purely utility-based.

Full Transparency

All flows publicly verifiable. Revenue, payments, and Korami distribution.
